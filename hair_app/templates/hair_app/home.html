Why Hair Donation Matters

Your hair can bring back confidence and hope

Cancer Patients

Chemotherapy causes hair loss, affecting confidence. Your donation can help them feel like themselves again.

Burn Victims

Severe burns can result in permanent hair loss. Your hair can help restore their appearance and self-esteem.

Medical Conditions

Alopecia and other conditions cause hair loss. Your donation gives them hope and confidence.

How It Works

For Donors
  1. Register: Fill out the donor registration form
  2. Requirements: Hair should be at least 8-10 inches long
  3. Get Matched: We'll match you with someone in need
  4. Donate: Visit a salon or donate from home
  5. Make a Difference: Change someone's life!
For Recipients
  1. Submit Request: Fill the hair request form
  2. Upload Documents: Medical certificate required
  3. Get Verified: Our team will review your request
  4. Get Matched: We'll find a suitable donor
  5. Receive Hair: Get your hair donation with love
